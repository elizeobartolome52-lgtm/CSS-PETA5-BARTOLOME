"DepEd presents plan for AI in education plan"

AUTHOR'S INSIGHTS

The Author thinks that this plan can potentially helps the country to improve its current education curriculum, but it can also worsen the critical thinking skills of students if not used properly.


BRIEF OVERVIEW

The Philippines’ Department of Education (DepEd) presented a national strategy to integrate artificial intelligence (AI) into basic education, outlining ethical frameworks, human-centered use of AI tools, and efforts to harness AI to support teaching and learning while ensuring human oversight and responsible deployment in schools.


"DepEd to co-build AI with DOST, vows safeguards vs student overreliance on AI generative tools"

AUTHOR'S INSIGHTS

The Author was impressed by the desissions that DEPED are currently making. The Author hopes that there is no controversy about this collaboration.


BRIEF OVERVIEW

DepEd announced a plan to collaborate with the Department of Science and Technology (DOST) to develop AI tools for education while committing to safeguards against student overreliance, emphasizing that AI should complement traditional learning and maintain ethical, transparent use through student disclosure and human supervision.


"AI is a tool, not a teacher: DepEd introduces guidelines on the use of AI in education"

AUTHOR'S INSIGHTS

The Author thinks that these guidelines helps the current curriculum to prevent the improper usage of AI Tools to cheat on school works.


BRIEF OVERVIEW

DepEd introduced comprehensive national guidelines on AI use in basic education, declaring that AI should assist but not replace human teachers, requiring students to disclose AI use, prohibiting high-risk applications like biometric profiling, and grounding AI deployment in ethical principles to preserve critical thinking and student integrity.
